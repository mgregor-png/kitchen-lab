# Sweet Potato, Minced Beef & Cottage Cheese Bowl

Serves 2

## Method

1. Peel and dice the sweet potato into small chunks. Spray with oil and season with salt and pepper.

2. Cook in the air fryer at 200°C for 16–18 mins until golden and soft. Alternatively, roast in the oven at 180°C for 30 mins.

3. Meanwhile, set a non-stick frying pan over a medium-high heat. Add the minced beef and cook for 6–7 mins until browned and cooked through. Stir in the taco seasoning and a pinch of salt.

4. While that’s cooking, halve the cherry tomatoes and shred the iceberg lettuce.

5. To plate up, spoon the roasted sweet potatoes into bowls, followed by the cottage cheese and taco beef. Add the lettuce, tomatoes and pickled onions.

6. Drizzle over the hot honey and finish with a pinch of chilli flakes if you like it spicy.

## Ingredients

### Beef
- 260g Sweet Potato
- 300g 5% Fat Beef Mince
- 2tbsp Good-Quality Mild Taco Seasoning
- 160g Cottage Cheese
- 150g Cherry Tomatoes
- 150g Iceberg Lettuce
- 2tbsp Pickled Red Onion
- Salt

### To Serve
- 30g Hot Honey
- Chilli Flakes (optional)

### Other
- 5g Vegetable Oil Spray
- Black Pepper
