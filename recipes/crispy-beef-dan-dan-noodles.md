# Crispy Beef Dan Dan Noodles

Serves 2

## Method

1. Start with the prep for your mince. Grate your ginger and slice the spring onions, set aside the green parts for garnish later. 

2. Heat the oil in a pan over medium heat and fry the mince for 8 minutes, until the water has evaporated and the fat starts to render and sizzle.

3. Add the ginger and white parts of the sliced spring onion. Sauté for a couple of minutes until fragrant. Pour in the Shaoxing wine and cook for 2 mins, until the alcohol is cooked off. Then season with the light soy sauce, dark soy sauce and sugar. Keep on low heat so it stays warm.

4. Grate your garlic into a small bowl and add the remaining sauce ingredients, mixing everything together until smooth.

5. Boil the noodles according to packet instructions and add the choy sum for the final 45 seconds of cooking.

6. Toss the noodles with the sauce, loosening with a splash of noodle cooking water if needed. Plate with the choy sum and cooked mince, then finish with the green sliced spring onions, crushed peanuts and extra crispy chili oil, if you fancy.

## Ingredients

- 400g Beef Mince
- 2tbsp Neutral Oil
- 2 Spring Onion
- 10g Fresh Ginger
- 2tbsp Shaoxing Wine
- 1tsp Light Soy Sauce
- .5tsp Dark Soy Sauce
- 1tsp Sugar

- 2Clove Garlic
- 45g Peanut Butter
- 1tbsp Light Soy Sauce
- 1tbsp Dark Soy Sauce
- 1tbsp Crispy Chilli Oil
- 1tbsp Chinese Black Vinegar
- .5tsp Sugar (optional)

- 2Nest Fresh Wheat Noodles
- 200g Choy Sum
- 30g Peanut
- Crispy Chilli Oil
