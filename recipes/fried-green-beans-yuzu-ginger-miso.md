# Fried green beans with yuzu ginger & miso

## Method
1. Place a large non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add the sesame seeds. Toast for about 3 minutes, mixing often, until golden brown. Transfer to a mortar and pestle. Add the sugar and a pinch of salt, and pound into a coarse crumb. Set aside.

2. Place the same pan over high heat. When hot, add 1 tablespoon of oil and half the beans. Fry for about 4 minutes, colouring the beans on all sides. Remove the beans to a large bowl, and repeat with the rest of the beans. Return the pan to high heat and cook the spring onions for 1 minute until starting to brown. Add them to the bowl along with  1/2 teaspoon salt and toss to combine. Add the yuzu marinade and coriander and mix well.

3. To serve, pile the beans onto a plate and sprinkle over the sesame seed mix.

## Ingredients
- 10g sesame seeds
- ⅛ tsp caster sugar
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 pack green beans, trimmed (350g)
- 2 spring onions, thinly sliced on an angle (40g)
- 40g Ottolenghi Yuzu, Ginger, and Miso Marinade
- 10g coriander leaves, roughly chopped
- fine sea salt
