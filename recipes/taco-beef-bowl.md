# Taco Beef Bowl with Sweet Potato & Whipped Goat's Cheese

## For the Bowl

- 260g sweet potatoes
- 300g 5% fat beef mince
- 2 tbsp mild taco seasoning
- 150g cherry tomatoes
- 150g iceberg lettuce
- 2 tbsp pickled red onions
- 5g non-stick vegetable oil spray
- 80g Bettine goat's cheese pearls (for whipping)
- 1–2 tbsp natural yoghurt (to loosen)
- Salt and pepper

## To Finish

- 20g Bettine goat's cheese pearls (whole)
- 30g hot honey
- Chilli flakes (optional)

## Steps

**Sweet potato:**
- Peel and dice into small chunks
- Spray with oil, season with salt and pepper
- Air fryer: 200°C for 16–18 minutes until golden and soft
- Alternatively: oven at 180°C for 30 minutes

**Taco beef:**
- Set a frying pan over medium-high heat
- Cook minced beef for 6–7 minutes until browned and cooked through
- Stir in taco seasoning and a pinch of salt

**Whipped goat's cheese:**
- Add 80g goat's cheese pearls and yoghurt to a bowl
- Season with salt and pepper
- Whip until smooth and creamy

**Assembly:**
- Halve cherry tomatoes, shred iceberg lettuce
- Spoon whipped goat's cheese into base of bowl
- Top with roasted sweet potato and taco beef
- Add lettuce, tomatoes and pickled onions
- Drizzle hot honey over the top
- Finish with remaining goat's cheese pearls and chilli flakes
