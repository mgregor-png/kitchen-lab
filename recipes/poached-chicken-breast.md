# Poached Chicken Breast

Serves 6

## Method

1. Pop the water, spring onions, garlic cloves and salt in a large saucepan, set over a high heat and bring to the boil.

2. Once boiling, throw in the chicken breasts, bring back to a simmer, pop the lid on and turn off the heat. Leave untouched for 25 mins. The residual heat will cook the chicken. 

3. Using tongs, remove the chicken from the liquid and set aside to cool. Once cool enough to handle, slice the chicken.

4. Divide between Tupperware containers with your favourite base, side and dressing and store in the fridge until needed.

## Ingredients

- 2L Water
- 2 Spring Onion
- 2 Garlic Clove
- 1tbsp Fine Salt
- 6 Chicken Breast
