# Balsamic & Fennel Italian Eggs

Serves 6

## Method

1. Bring a saucepan of salted water to the boil. Gently lower in the eggs and boil for 6 mins. Cool in iced water and peel once chilled.

2. Toast the fennel seeds in a dry pan, then grind in a pestle and mortar. Add the garlic and smash together.

3. Finely chop the chives, slice the chilli and zest the lemon.

4. In a bowl mix together the lemon zest, red chilli, chives, crushed fennel and garlic, oregano, balsamic vinegar, olive oil, salt and pepper. 

5. Place the peeled eggs in a clean jar or container. Pour over the marinade and top up with a little extra olive oil to fully cover.

6. Chill for at least 4 hrs, ideally overnight. The oil will separate, totally normal. You’ll end up with half the egg deeply brown and vinegared. Really delicious.

7. Serve halved with a spoonful of marinade, crusty bread and something salty.

## Ingredients

- 6 Egg

- 200ml Olive Oil
- 150ml Balsamic Vinegar
- 1tbsp Fennel Seed
- 3Clove Garlic
- 10g Chive
- 1 Lemon
- 1 Red Chilli
- 1tbsp Dried Oregano
- 10g Salt
- Black Pepper
