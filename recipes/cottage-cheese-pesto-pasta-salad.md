# Cottage Cheese Pesto Pasta Salad

Serves 2

## Method

1. Add the cottage cheese and all the basil leaves to a blender with water. Grate in most of the Parmesan, saving some for finishing later. Blitz until smooth and bright green. Add a splash of water to loosen it if needed. 

2. Halve the cherry tomatoes.

3. Cook the fusilli in a pan of salted boiling water until al dente. Drain, reserving a ladle of pasta water.

4. Return the pasta to the pan and toss over a low heat with the green sauce, spinach, tomatoes and a splash of the reserved pasta water to help it all come together.

5. Divide between two bowls and finish with the remaining Parmesan and a crack of black pepper.

## Ingredients

- 250g Cottage Cheese
- 30g Fresh Basil
- 30g Parmesan

- 160g Fusilli
- 200g Cherry Tomatoes
- 40g Baby Spinach
- Salt
- Black Pepper
