# Spicy Sriracha Salmon

Serves 4

## Method

1. First, grate your garlic cloves. Then, in a large bowl, whisk together all the sauce ingredients. Cut the salmon into large cubes (about 3 per fillet) and finely slice the spring onions.

2. Add the salmon cubes to the bowl and toss well to coat. Transfer to a container and leave to marinate in the fridge for at least 10 mins.

3. Preheat your air fryer to 240°C.

4. Add the marinated salmon to the air fryer basket and cook for 3-4 mins, until caramelised and cooked through.

5. Serve with a sprinkle of sesame seeds, sliced spring onions, and lemon wedges.

## Ingredients

- 1000g Skinless Salmon Fillet
- 4 Garlic Clove
- 2tbsp Fish Sauce
- 3tbsp Miso
- 3tbsp Sriracha
- 2tbsp Honey
- 1tbsp Rice Vinegar
- 2 Spring Onion

- Sesame Seeds (to serve)
- Lemon Wedge (to serve)
