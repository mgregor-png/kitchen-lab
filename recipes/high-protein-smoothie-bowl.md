# High-Protein Smoothie Bowl

Serves 2

## Method

1. Add all the ingredients to a blender. Blend until smooth and creamy. If you prefer a thinner consistency, add a splash of extra almond milk. Squeeze in some lemon juice for a little extra zing and pour it out into a bowl.

2. Finish with your toppings. We've gone for some fruit, granola, peanut butter, a drizzle of honey and more chia seeds for extra crunch. But add whatever you fancy.

## Ingredients

- 200ml Almond Milk
- 50g Vanilla Protein Powder
- 350g Skyr Natural Yoghurt
- 100g Blueberry
- 100g Raspberry
- 100g Pineapple
- 10g Chia Seed
- 10g Whole Flaxseed
