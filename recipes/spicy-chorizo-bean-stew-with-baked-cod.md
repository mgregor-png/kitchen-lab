# Spicy Chorizo & Bean Stew with Baked Cod

Serves 2

## Method

1. Cut the chorizo into 1cm cubes. Dice the red onion and thinly slice the garlic.

2. Add the chorizo to a cold medium saucepan and place over a medium heat. Cook gently for 5–6 mins until the fat has rendered and the chorizo is crispy. Remove with a slotted spoon and set aside, leaving the fat in the pan.

3. In a small bowl, mix half of the paprika with the olive oil and a good pinch of salt. Rub this all over the cod fillets. Place on a lined tray and roast at 190°C for 8–10 mins until just cooked through and flaky.

4. Back to the pan: add the onion, garlic and the remaining paprika. Cook for 3–4 mins, stirring occasionally, until softened and starting to caramelise.

5. Pour in the tinned tomatoes and chuck in the kale. Simmer for 10 mins until slightly thickened.

6. Add the butter beans along with water to loosen. Simmer for 2–3 more mins to warm everything through, then stir in half of the crispy chorizo. Squeeze in a little lemon juice and season to taste.

7. Divide the stew between bowls and top with the roasted cod. Finish with the remaining chorizo, a dollop of yoghurt and a handful of chopped parsley.

## Ingredients

### Cod
- 200g Cod Fillet
- 70g Chorizo
- 1x 400g Can Chopped Tomatoes
- 10g Olive Oil
- 2tsp Paprika
- 10g Fresh Parsley
- Salt

### Other
- 1 Red Onion
- 2Clove Garlic
- 40g Kale
- 1x 400g Can Butter Beans
- 200ml Water
- 1 Lemon
- 60g 0% Fat Greek Yoghurt⁣
