# Supergreen Matcha Smoothie Bowl

Serves 1

## Method

1. Chuck the banana, mango, pineapple, spinach, matcha, protein powder, almond milk, skyr, honey and chia seeds into a blender. Blitz until completely smooth and creamy – you want it super thick, so use a tamper or stop and stir between blends if needed.

2. Scoop the smoothie into a bowl. Top with sliced kiwi, strawberries, a sprinkle of desiccated coconut and some granola, if using.

## Ingredients

- 100g Frozen Banana
- 75g Frozen Mango
- 75g Frozen Pineapple
- 30g Spinach
- 1tsp Matcha
- 30g Vanilla Protein Powder
- 40ml Almond Milk
- 150g Skyr Natural Yoghurt
- 1tsp Honey
- 10g Chia Seed

- 1 Kiwi
- Strawberry
- Desiccated Coconut
- Granola
