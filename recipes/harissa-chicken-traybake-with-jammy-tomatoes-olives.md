# Harissa Chicken Traybake with Jammy Tomatoes & Olives

Serves 2

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 220°C. 

2. Tip the cherry tomatoes and olives onto a large baking tray. Drizzle over the olive oil, season with salt, black pepper and the oregano, then toss to coat. Nestle the chicken thighs skin-side up on top and season well with salt and black pepper, then drizzle over a little extra olive oil to help the skin crisp up.

3. Roast for 35–45 mins until the chicken is golden, the tomatoes have burst and everything's bubbling. If the skin needs extra colour, slide it under the grill for 2–3 mins at the end.

4. Meanwhile, microwave the wild rice pouch according to the packet instructions. Stir the yoghurt and harissa together in a small bowl. Squeeze in the lemon juice and add a pinch of salt.

5. Spoon the cooked rice into bowls and top with the chicken and all those jammy tomato and olive tray juices. Finish with generous dollops of harissa yoghurt and lots of torn parsley.

## Ingredients

- 4 Bone-In Skin-On Chicken Thigh
- 200g Cherry Tomatoes
- 100g Pitted Green Olive
- 1tbsp Olive Oil
- 1tsp Dried Oregano
- Salt
- Black Pepper

- 200g Greek Yoghurt
- 2tsp Harissa Paste
- .25 Lemon
- 1Pinch Salt

- 1x 250g Pouch Microwaveable Wild Rice Pouch
- 10g Fresh Parsley (to serve)
