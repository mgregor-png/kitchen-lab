# Blueberry & Almond Smoothie Bowl

Serves 1

## Method

1. Chuck the blueberries, blackberries, banana, milk, yoghurt, protein powder, almond butter, the juice of the lime and chia seeds into a blender.

2.  Start blending on a low speed. Use a tamper to push everything down – it’ll be thick. Add 10ml more milk at a time only if it really won’t blend.

3.  Scoop into a bowl and load up with your favourite toppings – fresh berries, granola, almonds, seeds or another drizzle of almond butter.

## Ingredients

- 100g Frozen Blueberries
- 50g Frozen Blackberry
- 50g Frozen Banana
- 40ml Almond Milk
- 60g Skyr Natural Yoghurt
- 30g Vanilla Protein Powder
- 15g Almond Butter
- 10g Chia Seed
- 1 Lime
- Fresh Berries
- Granola
- Flaked Almond
- Almond Butter
- Chia Seed
