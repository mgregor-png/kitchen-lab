# Green herb salad

## Method
1. Start with the dressing. Place the olive oil and sage in a small pan and put on medium heat. Remove as soon as the sage begins to sizzle and leave aside to cool. Remove the sage leaves, transfer the oil to a bowl and whisk the infused oil together with the lemon juice, 1/4 teaspoon of salt and 1/4 teaspoon of black pepper.

2. Place all of the herbs, leaves and mange tout in a large bowl. Scatter over the seed mix by hand. Just before serving, pour over the dressing. Lightly toss everything together and serve immediately.

## Ingredients
- 40g coriander leaves, picked 
- 20g basil leaves, torn 
- 40g parsley leaves, very roughly chopped 
- 15g picked dill
- 20g mint leaves, torn 
- 35g purslane or lambs lettuce, torn 
- 60g rocket, torn 
- 100g mange tout, finely sliced 
- 30g flaked almonds, lightly toasted with 1/2 a tbsp olive oil 
- 30g toasted pumpkin seeds
- 1/2 tsp  nigella seeds
- 15g toasted sesame seeds 
- 1/2 tsp chilli flakes
- 45ml olive oil 
- 6 sage leaves 
- 2 tbsp lemon juice 
- salt and black pepper
