# Berry Pink Power Smoothie Bowl

Serves 1

## Method

1. Chuck the frozen strawberries, raspberries, cherries and banana into a blender with the almond milk, yoghurt, protein powder, lime juice and chia seeds.

2. Start blending on a low speed. Use a tamper to push the mixture down towards the blades – it’ll take a minute to get going. Don’t add more liquid unless you absolutely have to. If it really won’t budge, splash in 10ml more almond milk at a time.

3. Scoop your thick smoothie into a bowl, then top with whatever you fancy – sliced fruit, coconut flakes, granola, seeds or a drizzle of almond butter.

## Ingredients

### Yoghurt
- 100g Frozen Strawberry
- 75g Frozen Raspberry
- 50g Pitted Frozen Cherries
- 100g Frozen Banana
- 40ml Almond Milk
- 60g Skyr Natural Yoghurt
- 30g Vanilla Protein Powder
- 10ml Lime Juice
- 10g Chia Seed
- Chia Seed
- Almond Butter

### Smoothie
- Coconut Flake
- Granola

### Other
- Strawberry
