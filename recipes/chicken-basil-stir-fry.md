# Chicken & Basil Stir Fry

Serves 4

## Method

1. Cook the rice following packet instructions.

2. Make the sauce: Add all the ingredients to a food processor and blend until smooth (don’t worry too much, some texture is nice). Taste and season with fish sauce. Set aside. 

3. Stir-fry time: Place a large pan or wok over a high heat and add the vegetable oil. Once hot add the chicken mince and cook it for 3 mins, breaking it up into small pieces with a wooden spoon as you go. Add the sauce and cook for another 2-3 mins.

4. Chop the broccoli up into bite-sized pieces. Add the broccoli to the pan and cook for 3-4 mins or until tender. Add the basil leaves and toss through to wilt. Taste and season with fish sauce, if needed 

5. For the eggs, either boil them for 7 mins then peel and store them in the fridge until serving, or fry them fresh each time you eat a portion. Nothing beats a crispy fried egg.

6. To store the portions, divide the rice evenly between containers and top with the chicken mince. Keep in the fridge for up to 4 days. Reheat in the microwave for 3 mins or until piping hot.

7. To serve, transfer the reheated rice and chicken to a plate and finish with an egg, a lime wedge and some sriracha, if you like.

## Ingredients

- 2 Shallot
- 5 Garlic Clove
- 2 Red Chilli
- 30g Caster Sugar
- 3tbsp Oyster Sauce (to serve)
- 3tbsp Dark Soy Sauce (to serve)
- Fish Sauce (to taste)

- 500g Chicken Mince
- 30ml Vegetable Oil
- 200g Tenderstem Broccoli
- 20g Thai Basil

- 200g Jasmine Rice

- 4 Egg
- 3.5tbsp Sriracha
