# Peanut Butter Overnight Oats, Three Ways

Serves 6

## Method

1. For the oats, combine all the oat ingredients in a mixing bowl and whisk together until well combined. Pour into an airtight container and refrigerate overnight.

2. For the yoghurt topping, add the yoghurt to a clean mixing bowl with the honey and almond milk. Whisk vigorously for about 2 mins until the mixture becomes shiny and slightly aerated. Pour into a separate airtight container and refrigerate overnight. The oats will keep for 4 days.

3. In the morning, serve up a portion of the oats into a bowl and top with the yoghurt. Finish with toppings of your choice: we love a scattering of crunchy nut cornflakes; a drizzle of honey and some grated frozen strawberries; or a sprinkling of cinnamon, sliced banana and a handful of granola.

## Ingredients

### Yoghurt
- 75g Crunchy Peanut Butter
- 180g Rolled Oat
- 280ml Unsweetened Almond Milk
- 25g Honey
- 40g Honey
- 60ml Unsweetened Almond Milk
- Crunchy Nut Cornflakes (to serve)
- 1tsp Ground Cinnamon (to serve)
- Banana (to serve)
- 25g Granola (to serve)
- 2tsp Honey (to serve)
- 5 Frozen Strawberry (to serve)

### Other
- 225g 0% Fat Greek Yoghurt⁣
- 60g Vanilla Protein Powder
- 1tbsp Vanilla Extract
- 2tbsp Chia Seed
- 120g Blueberry
- 1tsp Salt
- 800g 0% Fat Greek Yoghurt⁣
