# Peri-Peri Chicken & Halloumi Flatbreads With Cabbage Slaw

Serves 2

## Method

1. Cut the chicken and halloumi into bite-sized cubes and toss with the peri peri seasoning, a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of salt.

2. Set a large frying pan over a medium heat. Once hot, add the chicken and cook for 6-8 mins until golden and cooked through. Remove to a plate, then add the halloumi to the same pan and cook for 2 mins on each side until golden.

3. Finely shred the cabbage using a knife or peeler. Chuck it into a bowl with the yoghurt, a good squeeze of lemon juice and a pinch of salt. Roughly chop the parsley and mix it through.

4. Toast the flatbreads in a dry pan until warm and slightly charred.

5. Load them up with a generous spoonful of slaw, then pile on the chicken and halloumi. Finish with a squeeze of lemon and a drizzle of hot sauce, if you like.

## Ingredients

- 75g Boneless Skinless Chicken Thigh
- 60g Halloumi
- 5g Peri Peri Seasoning
- 10g Olive Oil
- Salt

- 200g Red Cabbage
- 200g 0% Fat Greek Yoghurt⁣
- 1 Lemon
- 10g Fresh Parsley
- Black Pepper

- 2 Flatbread
- Hot Sauce (optional)
