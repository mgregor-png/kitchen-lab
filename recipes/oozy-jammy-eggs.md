# Oozy Jammy Eggs

Serves 6

## Method

1. Fill a medium saucepan with water to and bring to a rolling boil.

2. Use a spoon to gently lower in the eggs into the boiling water. Don’t overcrowd the pan or lower the heat.

3. Set a timer for 6 mins if the eggs are room temp, or 6 mins 30 secs if they’re cold.

4. While they boil, fill a bowl with cold water and ice. Once the timer goes, transfer the eggs to the ice bath for 3 mins to stop them cooking further.

5. Peel if you’re eating them now – run them under cold water as you go to make it easier. 
If you’re storing them, keep unpeeled eggs in the shell for up to 7 days in the fridge. Peeled eggs should be stored submerged in cold water in a sealed container – they’ll stay fresh for up to 4 days.

6. To reheat, pop a cold egg in a bowl of warm water for 5 mins. The yolk stays oozy and delicious.

## Ingredients

- 6 Egg
- Water
- Ice
