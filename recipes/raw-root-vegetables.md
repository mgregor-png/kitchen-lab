# Raw root vegetables

## Method
1. Place the celeriac and swede in a bowl. Add all the other ingredients and mix by hand - &amp;#39;massaging&amp;#39; the veg a little while you do so will help them absorb the flavours. Adjust the seasoning, and the amount of sugar and vinegar, to your liking. This salad will keep well in the fridge for two days. Add fresh herbs for a fresher look.

## Ingredients
- 250g celeriac, thinly shredded
- 250g swede, thinly shredded
- 20g flat-leaf parsley, chopped
- 20g dill, chopped
- 70g capers, chopped (plus 1 tsp of caper juices if preserved in liquid)
- 1 lemons juice
- 2 tsp cider vinegar
- 4 tbsp olive oil 
- 4 tbsp sunflower oil
- 3 tsp dijon mustard
- 2 garlic cloves
- 2 tsp sugar
- 100g dried sour cherries, whole
- salt and pepper
