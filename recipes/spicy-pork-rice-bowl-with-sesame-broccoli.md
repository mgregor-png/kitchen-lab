# Spicy Pork Rice Bowl with Sesame Broccoli

Serves 2

## Method

1. Finely grate the garlic and mix it with the gochujang, oyster sauce, dark soy sauce and sugar in a small bowl. Set aside.

2. Place a large frying pan over a high heat with the vegetable oil. Once hot, add the pork mince and cook for 5–7 mins, breaking it up with a spoon as you go, until golden and caramelised.

3. Pour in the sauce and stir through. Cook for another 2–3 mins until thick, glossy and sticky.

4. Meanwhile, microwave the tenderstem broccoli for 2 mins until just tender. Toss with a pinch of salt, the sesame oil and the sesame seeds. Alternatively, steam or pan-fry if you prefer.

5. Trim and thinly slice the spring onions.

6. Spoon the rice into bowls. Top with the pork, sesame broccoli and a scattering of spring onions and extra sesame seeds. Finish with a raw egg yolk, if using. 

## Ingredients

### Sauce
- 1Clove Garlic
- 1tbsp Gochujang
- 1tbsp Oyster Sauce
- 1tbsp Dark Soy Sauce
- 1tbsp Soft Light Brown Sugar

### Pork
- 300g 10% Fat Pork Mince
- 1tbsp Vegetable Oil

### Sesame Broccoli
- 200g Tenderstem Broccoli
- 1tsp Sesame Oil
- 1tsp Sesame Seeds
- Salt

### To Serve
- 300g Cooked Sushi Rice
- 2 Spring Onion
- Egg Yolk (optional)
