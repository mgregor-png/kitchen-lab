# Apple Crumble Overnight Oats

Serves 4

## Method

1. For the oats, dice or grate the apple, then add ½ the apple to a clean mixing bowl along with the remaining oats ingredients. Whisk together until well combined. Divide the mixture between containers, about 130g per container. Make sure your butter is fridge-cold.

2. For the yoghurt topping, add the yoghurt to a clean mixing bowl with the remaining topping ingredients. Whisk vigorously for about 2 mins until the mixture becomes shinny and slightly aerated. Add the remaining grated/diced apple and mix through. Gently spoon the mixture over the oats, about 100g per container. I spoon the yoghurt around the edge of the container first before adding to the middle to maintain a visual line between the oats and the topping, but that’s just aesthetic.

3. For the crumble, add all the ingredients into a clean bowl. Using your fingers, mix the ingredients together until you have no lumps of butter and a nice crumbly texture. Sprinkle over the top of each container.

4. Cover and place in the fridge overnight. The oats will keep for 3 days.

## Ingredients

- 1 Apple
- 120g Rolled Oat
- 180ml Unsweetened Almond Milk
- 15ml Maple Syrup
- 150g 5% Fat Greek Yoghurt
- 30g Vanilla Protein Powder
- 15ml Vanilla Extract
- 1tsp Ground Cinnamon
- 450g 5% Fat Greek Yoghurt
- 20g Brown Sugar
- 15ml Unsweetened Almond Milk
- 10g Unsalted Butter
- 25g Rolled Oat
- 10g Brown Sugar
- .5tsp Ground Cinnamon
