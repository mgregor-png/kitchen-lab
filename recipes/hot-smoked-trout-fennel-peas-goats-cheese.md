# Hot smoked trout with fennel, peas and goat's cheese 

## Method
1. Put the oil, lemon juice and dill in a large bowl, add half a

2. teaspoon of salt and some black pepper, and whisk. Add the fennel, stir

3. and leave to marinate for about 30 minutes. Put the cheese in another

4. bowl and mix in the fennel seeds, lemon zest and an eighth of a teaspoon

5. of salt.

6. To serve, add the pea shoots to the large bowl, flake the trout into

7. 5cm pieces and add these, too, with the peas and peppercorns. Mix

8. gently, transfer to a serving dish, dot cheese on top, drizzle with oil

9. and serve.

## Ingredients
- 75ml olive oil, plus extra to finish
- 2 tbsp lemon juice
- 5g picked dill leaves
- Salt and black pepper
- 1 large fennel bulb, trimmed and very thinly shaved
- 100g soft rindless goat&amp;#39;s cheese, broken into chunks
- 3/4 tsp fennel seeds, toasted and lightly crushed
- 1/2 tsp grated lemon zest
- 40g pea shoots
- 4 hot-smoked trout fillets
- 150g peas (frozen are fine), blanched for a minute, refreshed and drained
- 1 tsp pink peppercorns, crushed
