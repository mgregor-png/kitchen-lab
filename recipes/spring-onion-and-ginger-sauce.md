# Spring Onion and Ginger Sauce

Serves 4

## Method

1. Finely slice the spring onions and mince the fresh ginger. Add the finely sliced spring onions and minced ginger to a heatproof bowl.

2. Stir in the salt, MSG (if using), and sugar.

3. Heat the vegetable oil in a small pan over high heat until smoking.

4. Carefully pour the hot oil over the spring onion mixture, allowing it to sizzle and release its aroma.

5. Stir well and let sit for at least 5 minutes before serving.

## Ingredients

- 20 Spring Onion
- 30g Fresh Ginger
- 1tsp Salt
- 1tsp MSG (optional)
- 1tsp Sugar
- 100ml Vegetable Oil
