# Creamy Spinach & Pea Orzo with Cod

Serves 2

## Method

1. Finely chop the dill and parsley, reserving a few leaves for garnish. Peel and finely dice the shallots and garlic.

2. Set a large lidded frying pan over a medium heat with the olive oil. Once hot, add the shallots and garlic with a pinch of salt and cook for 3 mins until soft and translucent.

3. Tip in the orzo and stir to coat. Pour in the water, bring to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 5 -10 mins, stirring occasionally.

4. Add the spinach and peas to the pan and mix through until wilted.

5. Add your chopped herbs to the orzo, stirring to combine. Then season the cod fillets with salt and pepper, then nestle them into the orzo. Cover with a lid and cook on low for 4–5 mins, until the fish is opaque and flakes easily and the orzo is al dente.

6. Squeeze over the juice of half the lemon and stir gently. Taste and season as needed.

7. Serve the orzo in bowls, top with the cod, a dollop of yoghurt, the reserved herbs and the remaining lemon cut into wedges.

## Ingredients

- 200g Skinless Cod Fillet
- 130g Orzo
- 80g Spinach
- 80g Frozen Pea
- 2 Shallot
- 3Clove Garlic
- 10g Fresh Dill
- 10g Fresh Parsley
- 60g 0% Fat Greek Yoghurt⁣
- 1tbsp Olive Oil
- 320ml Water
- 1 Lemon
- Salt
- Black Pepper
