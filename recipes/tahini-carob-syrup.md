# Tahini with carob syrup

## Method
1. Pour the tahini into a shallow bowl and drizzle the syrup all over. Sprinkle the falkey salt and serve.

## Ingredients
- 250g tahini
- 2 tbsp carob syrup (can also use date or maple syrup)
- 1/4 tsp flakey salt.
