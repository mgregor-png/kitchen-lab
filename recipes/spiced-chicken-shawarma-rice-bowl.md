# Spiced Chicken Shawarma Rice Bowl

Serves 2

## Method

1. Heat the oven to 210°C and start with the veg prep. Slice the red onion into wedges, grate the garlic for the chicken and thinly slice the cucumber and chop the fresh parsley.

2. Add the red onion wedges and cherry tomatoes to a baking dray with a drizzle of olive oil and salt, tossing to coat. Roast for 20–25 minutes until softened and lightly charred at the edges. 

3. While the veg is cooking, get on with the chicken. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium-high heat. Add the chicken mince and fry for 10 mins, until browned, breaking it up well so it caramelises rather than steams (you can do this in batches as well to help the meat crisp up). 

4. Once the meat is golden and crispy, stir through the ground cumin, coriander, paprika, chilli flakes, grated garlic, harissa and salt. Fry for 5 mins, until fragrant and cooked through.

5. Grate in the remaining garlic for the sauce into a small bowl and squeeze in the juice of the lemon. Mix with Greek yoghurt, olive oil, and salt until smooth and creamy.

6. Mix the roasted tomatoes and onions with the julienned cucumber and chopped parsley. Add a sprinkle of salt and another squeeze of lemon juice.

7. Warm the rice and divide between two bowls. Spoon over the roasted tomato and onion salad. Top generously with the kofte-spiced chicken mince and finish with garlic yoghurt over the top.

## Ingredients

- 400g Chicken Mince
- 2Clove Garlic
- 1tbsp Olive Oil
- 1tbsp Ground Cumin
- 1tbsp Ground Coriander
- 1tbsp Paprika
- 1tsp Chilli Flakes
- 1tbsp Harissa Paste
- Salt

- 150g Greek Yoghurt
- 1Clove Garlic
- 1tbsp Olive Oil
- .25 Lemon
- Salt

- 2 Red Onion
- 200g Cherry Tomatoes
- 1 Cucumber
- 10g Fresh Parsley
- Olive Oil
- .25 Lemon
- Salt

- 300g Cooked Jasmine Rice
