# Tropical Sunshine Smoothie

Serves 1

## Method

1. Chuck the mango, pineapple, banana, coconut or almond milk, lime juice, turmeric, ginger and protein powder into a blender.

2. Blend until smooth and creamy. If it’s looking a bit thick, splash in some cold water to loosen.

3. Pour into a glass and get stuck in.

## Ingredients

- 75g Frozen Mango
- 75g Frozen Pineapple
- 50g Banana
- 160ml Coconut Milk
- 0.5 Lime
- 0.25tsp Ground Turmeric
- 5g Fresh Ginger
- 30g Vanilla Protein Powder
- 5ml Cold Water
