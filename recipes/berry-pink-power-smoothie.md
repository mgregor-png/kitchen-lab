# Berry Pink Power Smoothie

Serves 1

## Method

1. Add the strawberries, 50g of the raspberries, cherries, banana, almond milk, yoghurt and protein powder to a blender. Grate in the zest of the lime and then squeeze in the juice.

2. Blitz until smooth. Pour into a glass.

3.  Crush the remaining 25g frozen raspberries with the back of a spoon, then spoon over the top. Serve straight away – cold, creamy and berry-packed.

## Ingredients

- 100g Frozen Strawberry
- 75g Frozen Raspberry
- 50g Pitted Frozen Cherries
- 1 Banana
- 100ml Almond Milk
- 60g Skyr Natural Yoghurt
- 30g Vanilla Protein Powder
- 1 Lime
