# Kimchi Tuna Melt Wrap

Serves 4

## Method

1. Finely slice the spring onions, roughly chop the kimchi, and finely dice the celery. Add to a medium mixing bowl along with the drained tuna, cream cheese, mayo and sriracha. Mix everything together until well combined.

2. Finely slice the red onion using a mandoline or sharp knife. If you prefer a milder flavour, soak it in iced water for a few minutes, then drain well. Grate the Cheddar and set aside.

3. Choose your base – wraps, pitas, flatbreads, or even bread all work well.

4. Spread a layer of the tuna mix onto your chosen base, followed by a layer of red onion. Top with grated mozzarella and Cheddar.

5. Toast in a non-stick pan or pop in the air fryer for 6 mins until crispy and the cheese is melted. Serve immediately.

## Ingredients

- 6 Spring Onion
- 50g Kimchi
- 1Stalk Celery
- 4x 145 Can Tuna
- 75g Light Cream Cheese
- 70g Light Mayonnaise
- 50g Sriracha
- 1 Red Onion
- 4 Wholemeal Wrap
- 80g Mature Cheddar Cheese
- 100g Grated Low-Fat Mozzarella
