# Paprika Garlic Chicken Flatbreads With Whipped Feta

Serves 2

## Method

1. Peel and grate the garlic into a bowl. Stir in the paprika and Greek yoghurt. Cut the chicken into 2–3cm chunks and add to the bowl with a pinch of salt. Mix well to coat.

2. If using skewers, thread on the chicken and grill under a hot grill for 6 mins, flip and cook for another 2 mins until charred and cooked through. Alternatively, air fry at 220°C for 8-10 mins.

3. Finely slice the red onion and mix with a pinch of salt, lemon juice and sumac, if using. Set aside to pickle slightly.

4. Quarter the cherry tomatoes and chop the cucumber into bite-sized chunks. Add to the onions and mix well.

5. In a small bowl, crumble in the feta and stir through the remaining yoghurt until combined. Season to taste.

6. Toast the flatbreads in a dry pan or under the grill until warm and slightly charred.

7. To serve, spread the feta-yoghurt mixture over the flatbreads, pile on the paprika chicken, then spoon over the tomato salad. Finish with fresh parsley, mint, dried oregano and a lemon wedge.

## Ingredients

- 200g Chicken Breast
- 100g 0% Fat Greek Yoghurt⁣
- 1tbsp Paprika
- 3Clove Garlic
- Salt
- 100g Cherry Tomatoes
- 100g Cucumber
- 0.5 Red Onion
- 1tbsp Sumac (optional)
- 1 Lemon
- Salt
- 40g Feta
- 150g 0% Fat Greek Yoghurt⁣
- 2 Flatbread
- 10g Fresh Parsley
- 10g Fresh Mint
- 1tsp Dried Oregano
- 1 Lemon
