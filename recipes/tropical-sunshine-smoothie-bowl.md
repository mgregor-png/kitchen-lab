# Tropical Sunshine Smoothie Bowl

Serves 1

## Method

1. Chuck the mango, pineapple, banana, milk, yoghurt, juice of the lime, turmeric, ginger and protein powder into a blender.

2. Start blending on a low speed. Use a tamper to push the mixture down – it’ll be thick, but that’s what we’re after. Only add more liquid if you really need to. Splash in an extra 10ml of milk at a time if it won’t budge.

3. Scoop into a bowl and top with whatever you fancy – banana slices, coconut flakes, granola, seeds or a drizzle of almond butter.

## Ingredients

- 50g Frozen Mango
- 100g Frozen Pineapple
- 50g Frozen Banana
- 40ml Coconut Milk
- 60g Skyr Natural Yoghurt
- 1 Lime
- 0.25tsp Ground Turmeric
- 5g Fresh Ginger
- 30g Vanilla Protein Powder

- Banana
- Toasted Coconut Flake
- Granola
- Chia Seed
- Almond Butter
- Crushed Frozen Raspberries
