# Cauliflower, pomegranate and pistachio salad 

## Method
1. Preheat the oven to 200 degreesC fan.

2. Coarsely grate a third of the cauliflower and set aside in a bowl. Break the remaining cauliflower into florets, roughly 3cm wide, and add these to a separate bowl with the cauliflower leaves, if you have any, and onion. Toss everything together with 2 tablespoons of oil and 1/4 teaspoon of salt, then spread out on a large parchment-lined baking tray. Roast for about 20 minutes, until cooked through and golden-brown. Remove from the oven and set aside to cool.

3. Once cool, put the roasted vegetables into a large bowl with the 50ml oil, the grated cauliflower and the remaining ingredients, along with 1/4 teaspoon of salt. Toss gently, just to combine, then transfer to a platter and serve.

## Ingredients
- 1 large cauliflower (800g)
- 1 medium onion, roughly sliced (130g)
- 80ml olive oil
- 25g parsley, roughly chopped
- 10g mint, roughly chopped
- 10g tarragon, roughly chopped
- seeds from 1/2 medium pomegranate (80g)
- 40g pistachio kernels, lightly toasted and roughly chopped
- 1 tsp ground cumin 
- 11/2 tbsp lemon juice 
- salt
