# Ottolenghi Advocaat

## Method
1. Advocaat:

2. In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar, yolks and vanilla. Stream in the cream and whisk until combined. Lastly, stream in the rum and whisk until combined.

3. Strain through a fine mesh sieve into a jug and pour into sterilised glass jars or small bottles and keep refrigerated for up to 2 months.

4. Snowball sundae:

5. Dollop a spoonful of whipped cream in the base of a small dessert dish or ice cream coupe. Place a scoop of lemon sorbet on top and pour over 20cl or so of advocaat. Finish with a generous sprinkle of crushed meringue and a grating of nutmeg, if using.

## Ingredients
- 500g icing sugar
- 12 best quality egg yolks
- 1 vanilla bean, scraped (or 1 tsp vanilla extract)
- 300ml double cream
- 500ml light gold rum
- freshly grated nutmeg, to serve (optional)
- whipped cream, unsweetened
- lemon sorbet, shop bought
- Advocaat
- meringues, shop bought, crushed
- freshly grated nutmeg (optional)
