# Sweet and smokey chicken wings with spring onions and lime

## Method
1. Preheat the oven to 200C.

2. Add the chicken to a  large parchment lined tray along with the marinade, 75ml of water and 3/4 teaspoon of salt. Mix well, making sure the wings are in a single layer, and roast for 45 minutes until slightly charred and the sauce has thickened.

3. Scatter over the spring onions and serve with a squeeze of lime.

## Ingredients
- 1 kg chicken wings
- 1 jar Ottolenghi Smokey Honey marinade
- 6-7 spring onions, thinly sliced in rounds (60g)
- lime wedges, to serve
- fine sea salt
