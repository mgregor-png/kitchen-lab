# Poached Chicken & Ginger Broth

Serves 6

## Method

1.  Pop the chicken into a large pot and cover with cold water or chicken stock, if you’ve got some knocking about. Chop the spring onions into big chunks. Roughly slice the ginger and smash the garlic. Chuck it all into the pot with the salt.

2. Set the pot over a high heat and bring it up to a proper rolling boil. As soon as it's bubbling, turn off the heat, whack a lid on and leave it to poach gently in the hot liquid for 90 mins.

3. After 90 mins, take off the lid and remove the chicken. Leave it to rest at room temperature for at least another 30 mins. Carve off the breasts and shred the legs. 

4. Strain the broth into a bowl or jug, chucking out the spring onions, ginger and garlic. Don’t waste that broth, it’s gold. Perfect for sipping or spooning over rice.

## Ingredients

- 1 Large Whole Chicken
- 6 Spring Onion
- 60g Fresh Ginger
- 5Clove Garlic
- 2tbsp Salt
- 2L Water
- 1 Chicken Stock Cube
