# Gyros / Shawarma Meal Prep Guide

## Meat

- 600-800 g chicken thighs (or turkey / beef)
- 3 tbsp olive oil
- 3 tbsp Greek yogurt
- 4 cloves garlic
- 1 tbsp paprika
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp oregano
- salt, pepper
- juice of 1/2 lemon

**Steps:**
- Mix everything, marinate ideally overnight (min 1-2h)
- Cook hot pan or oven 200C ~20-25 min
- Aim for slight char

## Roasted Vegetables

- zucchini
- bell peppers
- red onion
- optional: sweet potato
- olive oil, salt, pepper, oregano

**Steps:**
- Chop chunky
- Toss with oil + spices
- Roast 200C ~25-30 min

## Greek Salad

- cucumber
- tomatoes
- red onion
- feta
- olives
- olive oil + salt

## Tzatziki

- 500 g Greek yogurt
- 1 grated cucumber (squeezed)
- 2 cloves garlic
- salt, pepper
- olive oil + optional dill

## Rice

- basmati or jasmine
- salt + olive oil

## Oven Cooking (Meat)

- 200-220C
- Single layer on tray
- 20-25 min
- Finish with grill 3-5 min

## Meal Prep Strategy

- rice bottom
- meat + roasted veg
- salad separate
- tzatziki separate

Freeze meat + veg + rice only.

- olives

- fresh dill
