# Supergreen Matcha Smoothie

Serves 1

## Method

1. Chuck the banana, mango, pineapple, spinach, matcha powder, protein powder, yoghurt, almond milk and honey into a blender. Add a handful of ice, if using, then blitz until smooth and creamy.

2. Pour into a glass and dust with a little extra matcha powder, if you fancy.

## Ingredients

- 1 Banana
- 75g Frozen Mango
- 75g Frozen Pineapple
- 30g Spinach
- 0.5tsp Matcha
- 30g Vanilla Protein Powder
- 150g Skyr Natural Yoghurt
- 75ml Almond Milk
- 1tsp Honey
- Ice
