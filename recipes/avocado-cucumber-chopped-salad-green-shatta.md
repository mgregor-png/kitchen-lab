# Avocado and cucumber chopped salad with green shatta

## Method
1. Mix everything together in a medium bowl, along with 1/4 teaspoon salt, and serve.

## Ingredients
- 250g mini cucumbers, finely diced
- 2 small avocados, roughly diced (215g)
- 1/2 red onion, finely diced (50g)
- 10g coriander, roughly chopped
- 10g mint, roughly chopped
- 3 tablespoons Ottolenghi green shatta
- 1 1/2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tsp lime juice
- fine sea salt
