# Spiced Brown Rice & Chickpeas

Serves 4

## Method

1. Cut the courgettes in half lengthways, then slice into half moons. Cut the red onions into large chunks.

2. Place a medium frying pan over medium heat and drizzle with olive oil.

3. Once hot, add the red onions, courgettes, smoked paprika, cumin, and coriander to the pan. Grate in the garlic and cook until softened, stirring occasionally. If anything catches on the pan, add a splash of water to deglaze.

4. Add the drained chickpeas and cooked rice to the pan. Stir everything together and cook for 2–3 minutes until heated through.

5. Season with salt, black pepper, and MSG (if using). Store in a Tupperware container along with your favourite protein, or by itself and pop in the fridge until needed. 

## Ingredients

- 500g Cooked Brown Rice
- 400g Drained Cooked Chickpeas
- 2 Courgette
- 2 Red Onion
- 1 Garlic Clove
- 1tbsp Smoked Paprika
- 1tsp Ground Cumin
- 1tsp Ground Coriander
- 1tsp MSG (optional)
- Olive Oil (to serve)
- Salt
- Black Pepper
