# Coriander and lime mayonnaise

## Method
1. Place all the ingredients in  the bowl of a food processor with ⅛ teaspoon of salt and blitz until smooth. Transfer to a serving bowl. Refrigerate if not using immediately.

## Ingredients
- 40g coriander
- 250g mayonnaise
- 3 tbsp lime juice
