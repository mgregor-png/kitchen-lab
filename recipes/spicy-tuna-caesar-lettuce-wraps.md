# Spicy Tuna Caesar Lettuce Wraps

Serves 2

## Method

1. Drain your tuna, then add to a large bowl along with the cottage cheese and yoghurt.

2. Finely chop the anchovy fillets and jalapeños and halve the cherry tomatoes. Grate the garlic and finely grate the Parmesan. Add to the bowl along with the Dijon, Worcestershire sauce, juice of the lemon and tear in the basil leaves. If you're using it, finely dice a little red onion and add that in too.

3. Mix everything together until creamy but still chunky. Season with salt and plenty of black pepper and stir until thick and scoopable.

4. Now you can assemble. Lay a large rectangle of parchment paper on your work surface. Arrange the lettuce leaves in two slightly overlapping rows of three, creating one large lettuce sheet. Dollop the smash in the middle and then roll from the short end up and over, like a burrito, then wrap tightly in the parchment, folding in the ends as you go. Slice in half and serve.

## Ingredients

### Sauce
- 3 Anchovy
- 1tsp Dijon Mustard
- 1 Garlic Clove
- 30g Parmesan
- 2tsp Worcestershire Sauce
- 30g Pickled Jalapeño
- .5 Lemon
- 100g Cherry Tomatoes
- 10g Fresh Basil
- 1 Red Onion (optional)

### Yoghurt
- 100g Tuna in Spring Water
- 100g Cottage Cheese
- 60g Greek Yoghurt

### Other
- 6Leaf Romaine Lettuce
- Salt
- Black Pepper
