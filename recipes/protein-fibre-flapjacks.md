# Protein & Fibre Flapjacks

Serves 6

## Method

1. Add the oats, protein powder, peanut butter, almond milk, flaxseeds, chia seeds, and mixed seeds to a large bowl. Mix everything together until you have a thick, cookie dough-like paste.

2. Line a small baking tray with parchment paper. Press the mixture firmly into the tray, smoothing the top with the back of a spoon.

3. Refrigerate for at least 3 hours, or until firm. Once chilled, slice into 6 bars or squares.

4. Eat as-is or jazz them up with your favourite toppings—try a dollop of yoghurt, some fresh berries, or a drizzle of melted dark chocolate and tahini.

## Ingredients

- 75g Rolled Oat
- 60g Vanilla Protein Powder
- 50g Smooth Peanut Butter
- 75ml Almond Milk
- 10g Ground Flaxseed
- 10g Chia Seed
- 50g Mixed Seeds
