# Avocado & Jalapeño Crema

Serves 4

## Method

1. Deseed and chop the jalapeños. Add the Greek yoghurt, jalapeños, avocado, half of the coriander, and lime juice to a blender.

2. Blend until smooth, scraping down the sides as needed.

3. Season with salt to taste.

4. Garnish with extra coriander and serve.

## Ingredients

- 250g 0% Fat Greek Yoghurt⁣
- 2 Jalapeño
- 1 Avocado
- 30g Fresh Coriander
- 2 Lime
- Salt
