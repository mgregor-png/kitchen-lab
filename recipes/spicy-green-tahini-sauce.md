# Spicy green tahini sauce

## Method
1. Combine everything in the small bowl of a food processor or blender, along with 115ml water and 1/2 teaspoon salt, and blitz until smooth and creamy.

## Ingredients
- 150g tahini
- 2 tbsp Ottolenghi green shatta
- 25g parsley, roughly chopped
- 15g dill, roughly chopped
- 1 garlic clove, roughly chopped
- 45ml lime juice
- fine sea salt
