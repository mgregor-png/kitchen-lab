# Sticky Cumin Lamb & Fresh Herb Salad

Serves 2

## Method

1. Start by prepping your veg for the mince. Finely chop the ginger, mince the garlic and slice the green chillis. Then slice the spring onions and shallots for the salad and set aside for later. 

2. Heat a pan over medium high heat and dry toast the cumin seeds for 30 to 60 seconds, until fragrant. Add the lamb mince and fry for 8-10 mins, breaking it up well, until browned and slightly crispy at the edges.

3. Stir through the ginger and garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant. Pour in the Shaoxing wine and let it cook off for a couple of mins, then add the dark soy sauce and oyster sauce. Season lightly with salt and cook until the lamb becomes sticky and glossy. Toss through the sliced green chillies. 

4. In a bowl, combine the coriander, sliced spring onions and shallots with the green chillies and squeeze over the juice of the lime along with a pinch of salt. Toss to combine, and your salad is ready.

5. Set a small frying pan over a medium heat and add a drizzle of olive oil. Crack in the eggs, and fry for 3-5 mins, until cripsy on the edges and the white is cooked and the yolk is still running. Sprinkle over some salt, and get ready to serve. 

6. Spoon the rice into bowls, top with the sticky cumin lamb and finish with a generous handful of the herb salad over the top.

## Ingredients

### Lamb
- 350g Lamb Mince
- 1tbsp Cumin Seeds
- 10g Fresh Ginger
- 1Clove Garlic
- 1tbsp Shaoxing Wine
- 1tbsp Dark Soy Sauce
- 1tbsp Oyster Sauce
- 2 Green Chilli 
- Salt

### Salad
- 15g Fresh Coriander
- 2 Spring Onion
- 2 Shallot
- .5 Lime

### To Serve
- 300g Cooked Jasmine Rice
- Egg
- 1tbsp Olive Oil
