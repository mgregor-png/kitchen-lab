# Olive & Herb Buttery Rice

Serves 4

## Method

1. Finely slice the garlic and chop the herbs. Wash the rice two or three times until the water runs clear.

2. Heat a medium-sized saucepan over medium heat and add the butter. Once melted, add the garlic and cook for 8 mins until caramelised.

3. Stir in the olives, a pinch of salt, and a pinch of sugar (if using). Cook for another minute.

4. Tip in the washed rice, then pour in the chicken stock (or water). Bring to a boil, then cover with a lid and reduce the heat to as low as possible.

5. Cook for 12 mins, then turn off the heat and let the rice sit for 15 mins.

6. Once ready, fluff the rice with a fork, mix through the chopped herbs, and season to taste with salt and black pepper. Serve immediately

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- 250g Jasmine Rice
- 300ml Chicken Stock

### Rice
- 4 Garlic Clove
- Salt
- Black Pepper

### Other
- 30g Salted Butter
- 20 Pitted Green Olive
- 20g Fresh Dill
- 20g Fresh Parsley
- Sugar (to serve)
