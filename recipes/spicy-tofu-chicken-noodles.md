# Spicy Tofu & Chicken Noodles

*By [@finntonry](https://www.tiktok.com/@finntonry)*

![Spicy Tofu & Chicken Noodles](images/spicy-tofu-chicken-noodles.jpg)

**Serves 2 | 587 kcal | 52g protein | 17g fat | 60g carbs**

## Chicken

- 300g chicken mince
- 2 garlic cloves, grated
- 1 inch ginger, grated
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp oyster sauce
- 2 tsp mirin

## Tofu Sauce

- 330g silken tofu
- 1 tbsp tahini
- 1 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tbsp miso
- 2 tsp rice vinegar
- 1 tbsp gochugaru

## Rest

- 1 cucumber
- 2 spring onions
- Coriander
- 350g frozen udon
- Sesame seeds (to finish)

## Steps

**Sauce:**
- Blend silken tofu, tahini, soy sauce, miso, gochugaru and rice vinegar until smooth
- Don't overblend or it goes runny — set aside

**Salad:**
- Julienne spring onions and pick coriander
- Slice cucumber into thin rounds, stack and cut into strips
- Mix cucumber with spring onion greens

**Chicken:**
- Add chicken mince to a hot pan over medium heat
- Let it colour before breaking up
- Deglaze with mirin, then add soy sauce and oyster sauce
- Cook for a few more minutes

**Assembly:**
- Cook udon noodles
- Plate: noodles on the bottom, pour over tofu sauce
- Chicken on one side, cucumber salad on the other
- Finish with sesame seeds
