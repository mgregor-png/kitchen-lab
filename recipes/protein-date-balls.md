# Protein Date Balls

Serves 10

## Method

1. Add all the ingredients to a food processor and blend until you have a fine breadcrumb texture.

2. Portion into equal-sized balls and squeeze them tightly into balls. Crush the peanuts.

3. Roll the balls in the crushed peanuts and finish with flaky salt. These will last up to a 7 days in the fridge and 3 months in the freezer.

## Ingredients

### To Serve
- 100g Natural Peanut Butter
- 30g Peanut
- 10g Peanut
- Flaky Sea Salt

### Other
- 100g Pitted Date
- 1tbsp Poppy Seed
- 40g Chocolate Chip
- 30g Vanilla Protein Powder
