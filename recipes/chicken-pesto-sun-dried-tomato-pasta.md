# Chicken, Pesto & Sun-Dried Tomato Pasta

Serves 2

## Method

1. Bring a pan of water to the boil and cook the pasta according to packet instructions.

2. Shred the cooked chicken and add to a large bowl with the cottage cheese and pesto. Mix until the chicken is well coated.

3. Finely chop the sun-dried tomatoes and spinach, quarter the cherry tomatoes, tear the basil and finely slice the spring onion. Fold everything through the chicken mixture, then season with salt and black pepper and mix until evenly combined and thick enough to scoop.

4. To serve, combine the chicken mix with the pasta and divide between bowls. Pop any leftover mix in the fridge, it will keep for up to 3 days.

## Ingredients

- 300g Cooked Chicken
- 250g Rigatoni
- 150g Cottage Cheese
- 60g Pesto
- 60g Sun-Dried Tomato
- 100g Cherry Tomatoes
- 20g Spinach
- 10g Fresh Basil
- 1 Spring Onion
- Salt
- Black Pepper
