# Salt & Pepper Roasted Chicken Thighs

Serves 4

## Method

1. Preheat the oven to 190°C.

2. Slice the onions and finely slice the garlic, then spread them evenly on a baking sheet.

3. In a large bowl, mix the salt, MSG (if using), sugar, five spice, white pepper, black pepper, garlic powder, cumin, and olive oil. Add the chicken thighs and toss to coat well.

4. Arrange the chicken thighs evenly over the onions on the baking sheet.

5. Bake for 30 minutes or until the chicken is golden, crispy, and cooked through.

6. Serve immediately with your favourite sides.

## Ingredients

- 8 Skin-On Chicken Thigh
- 3 Onion
- 3 Garlic Clove
- 1tsp Salt
- 1tsp MSG (optional)
- 2tsp Sugar
- 1tsp Chinese Five-Spice
- 1tsp Ground White Pepper
- 0.5tsp Black Pepper
- 0.5tsp Garlic Powder
- 0.5tsp Ground Cumin
- 2tbsp Olive Oil
