# Baked halloumi with fennel and carob syrup 

## Method
1. Preheat the oven to 200C.

2. Cut the top of the halloumi in a 1/2 cm wide criss cross pattern, taking care not to cut all the way through. Place the garlic, lemon peel and oregano in a 22cm baking dish and place the halloumi on top.

3. Pour over the oil and scatter over the fennel seeds. Bake for 15-17 minutes until golden in places and softened.

4. Serve with a pinch of salt on top and a drizzle of the carob syrup.

## Ingredients
- 1 block of halloumi (225g-250g)
- 1 garlic clove, bashed
- 1 lemon, peel removed with a peeler
- 15g oregano sprigs
- 45ml olive oil
- 1/2 tsp fennel seeds, lightly ground in a pestle and mortar
- carob syrup for serving
- flaked sea salt
