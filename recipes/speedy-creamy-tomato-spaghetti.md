# Speedy Creamy Tomato Spaghetti

Serves 2

## Method

1. Bring a large saucepan of salted water to the boil and cook the spaghetti following pack instructions until al dente. Reserve a mug of pasta water before draining.

2. Meanwhile, heat the olive oil in a frying pan over a medium heat. Add the pre-chopped garlic and cook for 30-60 seconds until fragrant – don't let it brown. Add the tomato purée and stir it through. Cook for 2–3 mins until it darkens and starts to smell sweet.

3. Add a splash of the reserved pasta water and stir until you've got a glossy, smooth sauce. Stir through the crème fraîche and let it bubble for about a minute until you've got a silky pink sauce. Season with salt, black pepper and chilli flakes, if using.

4. Add the drained pasta straight into the sauce and toss to coat, adding a splash more pasta water if it needs it. Stir through most of the Parmesan and tear in most of the basil.

5. Serve with the remaining Parmesan grated over and the rest of the basil scattered on top, and an extra dollop of creme fraîche if you fancy.

## Ingredients

- 200g Spaghetti
- 2tbsp Olive Oil
- 2tsp Pre-Chopped Garlic
- 2tbsp Tomato Purée
- 1Pinch Chilli Flakes (optional)
- 3tbsp Crème Fraîche
- 10g Fresh Basil
- 40g Parmesan
- Salt
- Black Pepper
- 5g Fresh Basil (optional)
- 1tbsp Crème Fraîche (optional)
