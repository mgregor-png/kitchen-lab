# Nuoc Cham (Vietnamese Fish Sauce Dressing)

Serves 4

## Method

1. Mince the garlic and ginger and finely chop the chillies.

2. In a small bowl, combine the minced garlic, ginger, and chopped chillies.

3. Add the fish sauce, rice vinegar, lime juice, sugar, and water.

4. Stir well until the sugar is fully dissolved.

5. Let sit for at least 5 mins to allow the flavours to meld.

6. Serve immediately or store in the fridge for up to a week.

## Ingredients

- 2 Garlic Clove
- 20g Fresh Ginger
- 2 Red Bird's Eye Chilli
- 5tbsp Fish Sauce
- 5tbsp Rice Vinegar
- 4tbsp Lime Juice
- 2.5tbsp Sugar
- 60ml Water
