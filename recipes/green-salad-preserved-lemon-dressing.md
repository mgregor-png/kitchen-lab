# Green salad with preserved lemon dressing

## Method
1. Place the nigella and sesame in a small pan over a medium heat for 3-4 minutes, stirring often, until lightly toasted.

2. In a large bowl, mix together the oil, preserved lemon marinade, yoghurt, lemon juice, sugar and 1 tablespoon of water. Add the lettuce and spring onions and toss to combine.

3. Transfer to a serving bowl, sprinkle over the seeds and serve.

## Ingredients
- 1/2 tsp sesame seeds
- 1/2 tsp nigella seeds
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 tbsp Ottolenghi Preserved Lemon Marinade
- 50g natural yoghurt or sour cream
- 1 tsp lemon juice
- pinch or two of sugar
- 3 baby gem lettuce, leaves picked (350g)
- 3 spring onions, finely sliced at an angle
