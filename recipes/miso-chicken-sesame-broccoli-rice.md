# Miso Chicken, Sesame Broccoli & Rice

Serves 2

## Method

1. Start by mixing the miso paste, rice vinegar and sugar in a bowl. Rub the mixture all over the chicken breasts and set aside to marinate while you prep everything else.

2. Rinse the rice 2–3 times under cold water until it runs clear. Add to a lidded saucepan with the water and a pinch of salt. Set over a medium heat. Once simmering, pop the lid on, reduce the heat to low and cook for 10 mins. Turn off the heat and leave to steam for another 10 mins. Fluff with a fork.

3. Cut the broccoli into bite-sized florets and place in a heatproof bowl with a splash of water. Cover and microwave for 2½ mins until tender. Drain and toss with the soy sauce and sesame seeds.

4. Heat the butter and vegetable oil in a large frying pan over a medium heat. Once sizzling, add the chicken breasts and cook for 4 mins on one side. Flip and cook for another 4–5 mins until golden and cooked through. Turn off the heat and leave to rest in the pan for 3–4 mins.

5. Slice the chicken and plate everything up — fluffy rice, sesame broccoli and juicy miso chicken. Spoon over any buttery pan sauce and finish with a lemon wedge.

## Ingredients

### Chicken
- 300g Chicken Breast
- 2tbsp White Miso
- 2tbsp Rice Vinegar
- 1tbsp Sugar
- 1tsp Vegetable Oil
- 10g Butter
- 100g Sushi Rice
- 150g Broccoli
- 1tbsp Soy Sauce
- 1tsp Sesame Seeds
- 1 Lemon

### Rice
- 120ml Water
- Salt
