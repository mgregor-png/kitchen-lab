# Lazy Cottage Cheese & Tomato Pasta

Serves 2

## Method

1. Bring a large saucepan of salted water to the boil and cook the pasta according to packet instructions until al dente. Reserve a mug of pasta water before draining.

2. Meanwhile, pour the tomato sauce into a frying pan and warm over a medium heat for 2–3 mins until bubbling. Add the cottage cheese and stir it through the sauce until it melts and turns creamy, adding a splash of pasta water if it's looking too thick. Toss in the spinach and let it wilt for 1–2 mins. Season with salt and plenty of black pepper.

3. Add the drained pasta to the pan and toss until evenly coated in the sauce, adding a little more pasta water to loosen if needed. Grate in most of the Parmesan and tear in most of the basil, stirring through.

4. Serve topped with the remaining Parmesan and basil.

## Ingredients

### Pasta
- 1x 350g Jar Tomato Pasta Sauce
- 300g Cottage Cheese
- 30g Spinach
- 15g Fresh Basil
- 50g Parmesan
- Salt
- Black Pepper

### Other
- 250g Conchiglie
