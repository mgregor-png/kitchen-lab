# Beef, beetroot and habanero stew

## Method
1. Season the beef with a teaspoon of salt. On a medium-high flame,

2. heat half the oil in a large, 28cm-diameter, heavy-based saucepan for

3. which you have a lid. Brown the beef in two or three batches, making

4. sure the pieces are spaced well apart, for seven minutes, turning

5. regularly so they colour on all sides, then remove the meat from the

6. pan.

7. Leave any juices in the pan and add the remaining two tablespoons of

8. oil, the garlic, shallots, bay leaves, orange peel and half a teaspoon

9. of salt. Saute for about four minutes, stirring from time to time, until

10. the onions are soft and golden, then add the beetroot and fry for

11. another four minutes, stirring occasionally, before returning the beef

12. to the pan. Pour over the beef stock, orange juice and water, and add

13. the tomato paste, habanero and ground spices. Bring to a boil, turn down

14. the heat to medium, cover and cook for two to two and a half hours,

15. until the meat is very soft and starting to fall apart and the sauce is

16. nice and thick. (If you're using a smaller pot, you may need to reduce

17. the sauce further to get the right consistency: you're looking for about

18. 350ml of thick sauce at the end.)

19. Serve on rice and top with a spoonful of soured cream, a sprinkle of coriander, if using, and a wedge of lime for squeezing over on the

20. side

## Ingredients
- 1kg boneless beef shin, cut into roughly 6cm square chunks
- Salt 
- 60ml olive oil
- 6 garlic cloves, peeled and roughly chopped
- 4 shallots, peeled and cut into 0.5cm-thick slices
- 3 bay leaves
- 3-4 oranges - shave the peel of 1 into four wide strips, then juice them all to get 400ml
- 500g beetroot, peeled and cut into 4cm chunks
- 500ml beef stock
- 1 litre water
- 11/2 tbsp tomato paste 
- 1/2 dried habanero chilli
- 11/2 tsp  ground cumin
- 11/2 tsp ground coriander
- 11/2 tsp ground cinnamon
- 160g soured cream
- 1 tbsp chopped coriander (optional)
- 1 lime quartered
