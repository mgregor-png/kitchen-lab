# Minced Beef & Broccoli Stir Fry

Serves 2

## Method

1. Mix all the sauce ingredients together in a bowl and set aside.

2. Mince the garlic and ginger. Cut the broccoli into bite-sized florets. Slice the spring onions and set aside.

3. Place the broccoli in a heatproof bowl with the water. Cover and microwave for 3 mins until just tender. Drain and set aside. Alternatively, blanch it in boiling water for 2 mins.

4. Place a wok or large frying pan over a high heat with the vegetable oil. Once hot, add the beef mince and stir-fry for 3–4 mins until browned.

5. Add the ginger and garlic to the pan and cook for 2 mins until fragrant.

6. Tip in the broccoli and pour over the sauce. Toss everything together for 2–3 mins until the sauce thickens and coats the beef and veg.

7. Drizzle over the sesame oil and toss again. Remove from the heat.

8. Serve topped with sesame seeds, the sliced spring onionsand extra white pepper if you fancy.

## Ingredients

- 250g 5% Fat Beef Mince
- 1tbsp Vegetable Oil
- 1tsp Fresh Ginger
- 3Clove Garlic
- 1 Broccoli
- 3tbsp Water
- 1tbsp Sesame Oil
- 2tsp Sesame Seeds
- 75ml Chicken Stock
- 2tbsp Light Soy Sauce
- 1tbsp Dark Soy Sauce
- 1tbsp Oyster Sauce
- 1tbsp Sugar
- 0.25tsp Ground White Pepper
- 1tbsp Cornflour
- 2 Spring Onion
