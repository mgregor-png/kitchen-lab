# Toasted Rice Sweet and Sour Dressing

Serves 4

## Method

1. Place a small pan over medium heat and add the jasmine rice. Dry toast for 4-6 minutes, stirring, until golden brown.

2. Transfer the toasted rice to a pestle and mortar and grind into a coarse powder (some texture is fine).

3. In a bowl, mix the chilli jam, fish sauce, and lime juice. Stir well to combine.

4. Add the ground toasted rice, finely sliced shallots, and chopped coriander to the dressing.

5. Mix everything together and let sit for 5 mins before serving.

## Ingredients

- 3tbsp Jasmine Rice
- 6tbsp Chilli Jam
- 3tbsp Fish Sauce
- 3 Lime
- 1 Shallot
- 15g Fresh Coriander
