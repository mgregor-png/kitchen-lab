# Cherry Tomato & Cottage Cheese Pasta Salad

Serves 4

## Method

1. If your pasta isn’t already cooked, bring a large pan of salted water to the boil and cook the fusilli following pack instructions. Drain, rinse under cold water and set aside to cool.

2. Halve the cherry tomatoes and finely slice the courgette. Grate the Parmesan. Toast the pine nuts in a dry frying pan over medium heat for 2–3 mins until golden. Tear the basil leaves. Shred or dice the cooked chicken.

3. Tip the cooled pasta into a large mixing bowl. Add the cherry tomatoes, sundried tomato paste, cottage cheese, chicken, courgette and most of the basil. Season generously with salt and black pepper, then toss everything together until well combined.

4. Divide between bowls or containers. Finish with the toasted pine nuts, a scattering of Parmesan and the rest of the basil.

## Ingredients

- 200g Cooked Fusilli
- 300g Cherry Tomatoes
- 250g Cottage Cheese
- 300g Cooked Chicken
- 2tbsp Sun-Dried Tomato Paste
- 50g Parmesan
- 20g Pine Nuts
- 15g Fresh Basil
- 1 Courgette
- Salt
- Black Pepper
