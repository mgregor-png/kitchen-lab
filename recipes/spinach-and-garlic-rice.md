# Spinach and Garlic Rice

Serves 4

## Method

1. Wash the rice two or three times until the water runs clear, then drain.

2. Add the spinach, water, garlic, and butter to a blender. Blend until smooth and vividly green.

3. Add the rice to a medium-sized saucepan and pour over the blended spinach mixture.

4. Bring to a boil, then cover with a lid and reduce the heat to as low as possible. Cook for 12 mins.

5. Turn off the heat and let the rice sit for 10 mins.

6. Remove the lid and fluff up the rice with a fork. Some spinach may settle on top – just mix it back through the rice.

7. Cover with the lid again and let sit for a further 5 mins. Season to taste with salt and black pepper before serving.

## Ingredients

- 250g Jasmine Rice
- 220ml Water
- 80g Spinach
- 1 Garlic Clove
- 20g Salted Butter
- Salt
- Black Pepper
