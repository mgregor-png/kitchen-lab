# Spicy Pork & Cabbage Stir-Fry

Serves 2

## Method

1. Grate the garlic and ginger into a small bowl. Add the sriracha, oyster sauce, soy sauces, sugar and black vinegar. Mix well to form your sauce.

2. Slice the spring onions, separating the whites and greens. Roughly chop the cabbage. Thinly slice the cucumber and pick the coriander leaves.

3. Soak the noodles in freshly boiled water for 90 seconds. Drain and rinse under cold water — they should still have a serious bite.

4. Set a large frying pan over a medium-high heat and add the sesame oil. Add the pork mince and cook for 4–5 mins until browned.

5. Pour in the sauce, add the spring onion whites and cabbage, and toss everything together over a high heat. Cook for another 3–4 mins until the cabbage softens and the sauce thickens slightly.

6. Add the noodles to the pan and toss to coat. Splash in a little water if it needs loosening.

7. Divide between two bowls and top with the cucumber slices, spring onion greens, coriander leaves and sesame seeds.

## Ingredients

- 3Clove Garlic
- 20g Fresh Ginger
- 2tbsp Sriracha
- 4tsp Oyster Sauce
- 4tsp Soy Sauce
- 1tbsp Dark Soy Sauce
- 2tsp Sugar
- 1tbsp Chinese Black Vinegar

- 250g 5% Fat Pork Mince
- 1 Hispi Cabbage
- 2 Spring Onion
- 100g Dried Rice Noodles
- 2tsp Sesame Oil

- 0.5 Cucumber
- 10g Fresh Coriander
- 1tsp Sesame Seeds
