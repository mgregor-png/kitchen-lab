# Leek-y Parmesan Chickpeas

Serves 4

## Method

1. Wash, trim, and slice the leeks in half lengthways, then cut into thin half-moons. Thinly slice the garlic.

2. Heat a little olive oil in a large frying pan over medium heat. Add the leeks and garlic, then cook for 8–10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until soft and jammy.

3. Stir in the Dijon mustard and chilli flakes, then cook for another minute.

4. Add the drained chickpeas and chicken stock, then bring to a simmer. Cook for 3–4 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the mixture thickens and becomes creamy.

5. Grate in the Parmesan and season to taste with salt, black pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice. Serve immediately.

## Ingredients

- 4 Leek
- 6 Garlic Clove
- 1 Lemon
- 1tbsp Chilli Flakes
- 1tbsp Dijon Mustard
- 480g Drained Cooked Chickpeas
- 200ml Chicken Stock
- 60g Parmesan
- Olive Oil (to serve)
- Salt
- Black Pepper
