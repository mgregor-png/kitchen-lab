# Tinned Tuna Poke Bowl

Serves 2

## Method

1. Drain the tuna and chuck it into a bowl with the mayo, sriracha and soy sauce. Mix well and set aside.

2. Peel and shred the carrots. Finely slice the red cabbage. Blanch the edamame in boiling water for 3 mins, then drain.

3. In a small bowl, whisk together the miso paste, rice vinegar, soy sauce and honey. Add a splash of water to loosen if it’s too thick.

4. Mix the carrot and cabbage with two-thirds of the miso dressing and toss to coat.

5. To build your bowls, start with a layer of warm rice. Top with the dressed slaw, spicy tuna and edamame beans.

6. Drizzle over the remaining dressing, sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve with lime wedges.

7. Tear up the nori and use it to scoop up all the good stuff.

## Ingredients

- 2x 165g Can Tuna
- 20g Light Mayonnaise
- 30g Sriracha
- 1tbsp Soy Sauce
- 2 Carrot
- 100g Red Cabbage
- 30g Edamame
- 1tbsp Miso
- 1tbsp Honey
- 1tbsp Soy Sauce
- 2tbsp Rice Vinegar
- 1 Lime
- 200g Cooked Sushi Rice
- 2Sheet Nori Seaweed
- 2tsp Sesame Seeds (to serve)
