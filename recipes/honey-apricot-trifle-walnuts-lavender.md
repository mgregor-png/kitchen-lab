# Honey and apricot trifle with walnut and lavender

## Method
1. Place the apricots in a small saucepan with the lemon juice, 2

2. tablespoons of water and 1 tablespoon of honey. Place on a medium heat

3. and cook for about 20 minutes, stirring from time to time, until the

4. apricots have broken down to a runny compote. You might need to add a

5. little bit more water if the apricots start sticking to the pan. Remove

6. from the heat and set aside to cool.

7. Place the cake in a bowl, pour over the liqueur and use your hands to gently toss the cake so that all the liquid is absorbed.

8. Put the walnuts in a bowl with the remaining half a tablespoon of honey and half of the lavender petals. Stir and set aside.

9. In a large bowl, whisk together the ricotta, creme fraiche, mascarpone and icing sugar. Once well combined, set aside.

10. Place some of the sponge at the bottom of 4 individual glass bowls.

11. Spoon some cream on top, followed by some of the compote and then the

12. nuts.  Repeat this layering process once more for each portion, drizzle

13. over the remaining honey and sprinkle with the remaining lavender

14. petals.

## Ingredients
- 8 apricots (390g gross), stoned and quartered (355g net) 
- 2 tbsp lemon juice 
- 11/2 tbsp good quality runny honey, plus an extra 1 tsp for drizzling 
- 200g Madeira cake (shop-bought is fine), broken up into 4cm chunks 
- 4 tbsp amaretto liqueur
- 100g walnuts, roughly chopped 
- 3/4 tsp picked lavender petals
- 100g ricotta 
- 150g creme fraiche 
- 50g mascarpone 
- 20g icing sugar
