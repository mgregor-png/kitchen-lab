# Chipotle Chicken Traybake

Serves 4

## Method

1. Preheat the grill to high.

2. Cut the chicken thighs into quarters and finely slice the onions.

3. In a large bowl, mix the chicken and onions with the chipotle paste, garlic powder, oregano, ground cumin, olive oil, salt, and black pepper. Toss to coat evenly.

4. Spread the mixture evenly on a baking tray.

5. Place under the grill on the top shelf and cook for 15-20 minutes, turning halfway, until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred.

6. Serve immediately with your favourite sides.

## Ingredients

- 1000g Boneless Skinless Chicken Thigh
- 3 White Onion
- 2tbsp Chipotle Paste
- 1tbsp Garlic Powder
- 1tbsp Dried Oregano
- 1tbsp Ground Cumin
- 2tbsp Olive Oil
- Salt
- Black Pepper
