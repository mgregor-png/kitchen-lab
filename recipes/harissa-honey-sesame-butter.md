# Harissa, honey and sesame butter 

## Method
1. Place the sesame seeds, spring onions and lemon zest in the small bowl of a food processor and blitz until broken down in smaller pieces. Add in the remaining ingredients and blitz until almost smooth.

2. Place into a bowl and drizzle over the reserved honey and sesame seeds.

## Ingredients
- 100g well toasted sesame seeds, plus extra for the top
- 5 spring onions (100g), trimmed and roughly chopped
- 1 lemon, zested, plus 1 tbsp juice
- 250g room temperature unsalted butter, cut into 2cm pieces
- 70g red harissa, (We used Cap du Bon Phare)
- 3/4 tbsp runny honey, plus extra to drizzle on top
