# Taco Beef Bowl Classic

Simple weeknight bowl - seasoned ground beef over rice with all the fixings.

## Meat

- 500 g ground beef
- 1 pack taco seasoning (or: 1 tbsp cumin, 1 tsp chili, 1 tsp paprika, 1/2 tsp garlic powder)
- splash of water

**Steps:**
- Brown beef in hot pan, break into crumbles
- Drain excess fat
- Add taco seasoning + splash of water, simmer 5 min

## Rice

- basmati rice
- salt + olive oil

## Toppings

- black beans (canned, drained and rinsed)
- sweet corn (canned, drained)
- cherry tomatoes, halved
- avocado, diced
- red onion, finely diced
- romaine lettuce, shredded
- jalapeno, sliced
- fresh cilantro
- cheddar cheese, grated or sliced

## Sauce

- sour cream
- lime juice
- (or mix sour cream + lime + pinch of salt for a crema)

## Assembly

- Rice on the bottom
- Beef on one side
- Line up toppings in sections
- Drizzle crema, squeeze lime, top with cilantro

## Ingredients

- 500 g ground beef
- basmati rice (500g)
- black beans, canned (1 can)
- sweet corn, canned (1 can)
- avocado (2)
- red onion (1)
- cherry tomatoes (1 pack)
- romaine lettuce (1 head)
- jalapeno (1 pack)
- fresh cilantro (1 bunch)
- cheddar cheese (150g)
- sour cream (200ml)
- lime (2)
- garlic (1 head)
- taco seasoning (1 pack)
