# Blueberry & Almond Smoothie

Serves 1

## Method

1. 
Chuck the blueberries, blackberries, banana, milk, protein powder, almond butter and chia seeds into a blender.

2. Blend until smooth. If it’s looking a bit too thick, loosen with a splash of cold water.

3. Pour into a glass and sip away.

## Ingredients

- 75g Frozen Blueberries
- 75g Frozen Blackberry
- 50g Banana
- 160ml Almond Milk
- 30g Vanilla Protein Powder
- 15g Almond Butter
- 10g Chia Seed
- 5ml Water
