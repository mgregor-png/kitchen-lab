# Roasted rhubarb with sweet labneh

## Method
1. Put the yoghurt in a bowl with the icing sugar and a

2. quarter-teaspoon of salt. Mix well and transfer to the middle of a clean

3. muslin or linen cloth. Tie into a bundle with an elastic band or

4. string, and hang over a bowl in the fridge for up to 18 hours.

5. Heat the oven to 180C/350F/gas mark 4. Cut the rhubarb into 6cm

6. batons and mix with the wine, sugar, vanilla pod and seeds, and lemon

7. strips. Put in an ovenproof dish that&amp;#39;s just large enough snugly to

8. accommodate the rhubarb and roast, uncovered, for 20 minutes, until

9. tender but not mushy. Set aside.

10. Just before serving, give the yoghurt a good squeeze to release the

11. last of the water. Remove from the cloth and place in a bowl. Stir in

12. the grated zest and spoon on to plates. Top with the rhubarb and some of

13. its cooking juices and sprinkle over the nuts.

## Ingredients
- 800g natural yoghurt
- 80g icing sugar
- Salt
- 400g rhubarb
- 100ml muscat (or other dessert wine) 
- 70g caster sugar
- 1/2 vanilla pod, scraped or 1/2 tsp vanilla paste 
- Skin of 1 lemon half-shaved into strips and the rest grated
- 20g pistachios, coarsely chopped
